Projects

The personification of my free time

GameCalc

An educational gamified calculator, 1st Place at HackRice 2014. Start with a limited number of buttons. Unlock new buttons on the calculator by doing math. Explore the definition of the functions and operations we take for granted.

WatchPoint

WatchPoint was inspired by the countless number of times I'd lost my phone. The app monitors the bluetooth signal between an Android phone and a Pebble smartwatch to be able to alert the user whenever the user leaves his or her phone behind.

Blackhole

A Christmas Day experiment of motion on polar coordinates. Written in javascript for canvas.

Amino Acids

Models of Amino Acids in 3 Dimensions written with THREE.js. An MCAT exam preparation book advised me to be familiar with the natural amino acids and their structure and this was my way of studying.

Higgs

This project is a subject of the old adage: The best things arise out of accidents. This is the product of a canvas experiment with particles after a line was accidentally deleted.
